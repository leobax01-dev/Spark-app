Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026
This policy explains what information SPARK collects, how it's used, and how you're in control of it — including data accessed through your connected Google account. It's written in plain language on purpose. If anything here is unclear, contact us at privacy@usesparkai.app.

1. Who we are

SPARK ("SPARK," "we," "us") is an AI-powered software platform for real estate agents, available at usesparkai.app. This policy applies to anyone who creates a SPARK account or otherwise uses the platform.

2. Information we collect

Account information

When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, and (if you subscribe to a paid plan) billing information processed securely by our payment provider, Stripe. We do not store your full payment card number.

Business data you provide

To power SPARK's features, you may enter information about your own clients, deals, pipeline, and business goals — for example client names, contact details, transaction notes, and deal values. You control what you enter, and you remain responsible for having the appropriate basis (such as an existing client relationship) to store and process that information about the people you work with.

Google account data (Gmail and Calendar)

If you choose to connect your Google account, SPARK requests read-only access to:

  • Gmail (read-only) — used to surface relevant email context (such as client replies) into SPARK's AI assistant and daily briefings.
  • Google Calendar (read-only) — used to reference upcoming appointments in your daily brief and AI conversations.
  • Basic profile info (email address) — used to confirm which Google account is connected.
SPARK's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Google data is used only to power the specific features described above — it is never sold, and it is never used to train third-party AI models beyond what's needed to generate your own in-app responses.

You can disconnect your Google account at any time from SPARK's Settings page. Doing so immediately revokes SPARK's access, and you can also revoke access directly from your Google Account permissions page.

Usage data

We collect basic product analytics (such as which features are used and how often) to improve SPARK. This is used in aggregate and is not sold to third parties.

3. How we use your information

  • To provide, operate, and improve SPARK's features, including AI-generated content, Autopilot intelligence, and business insights
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To send you service-related communications, including Autopilot alert emails you can configure or disable
  • To provide customer support
  • To maintain the security and integrity of the platform

4. Who we share information with

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with service providers who help us operate SPARK, under contracts that limit their use of it:

  • Anthropic — processes business data and conversation content to power SPARK's AI features (Claude API)
  • Supabase — hosts our application database
  • Stripe — processes payments and subscription billing
  • Resend — delivers transactional emails, such as Autopilot risk alerts
  • Vercel — hosts the SPARK application

We may also disclose information if required by law, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of SPARK, our users, or others.

5. Data storage and security

Your data is stored in encrypted databases with access restricted to authorized systems. Some data is also cached locally in your browser (localStorage) to make the app fast and to work briefly offline — this local cache is not a substitute for your account's data, which is stored securely in our database.

6. Data retention and deletion

We retain your account and business data for as long as your account is active, or as needed to provide the service. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting privacy@usesparkai.app. We will delete your data within a reasonable period, except where we're required to retain it for legal, tax, or accounting purposes.

7. Your choices and rights

  • Access, correct, or delete the business data you've entered directly within the app
  • Disconnect your Google account at any time from Settings
  • Opt out of non-essential email notifications from within the app
  • Request a copy of your data or its deletion by emailing us

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under applicable law (such as the right to data portability or to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority). Contact us and we'll do our best to help.

8. Children's privacy

SPARK is intended for licensed real estate professionals and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we'll notify you by email or through the app. Continued use of SPARK after changes take effect means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact us

Questions about this policy or your data? Email privacy@usesparkai.app.

This document is provided for general transparency and to meet third-party API disclosure requirements (including Google's API Services User Data Policy). It is not a substitute for legal advice — SPARK recommends having this policy reviewed by qualified legal counsel for your specific jurisdiction and business circumstances.
